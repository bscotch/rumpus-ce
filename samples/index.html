Rumpus CE is the community edition of the Rumpus API, which powers Levelhead and the broader Butterscotch Shenanigans universe.

Rumpus is designed, created, and maintained by Adam Coster, the CTO and co-founder of Butterscotch Shenanigans. He spends most of his time making Rumpus instead of playing Levelhead, so his stats aren't very impressive. But hey, you're here to see Rumpus CE in action, not to see impressive Levelhead stats!
